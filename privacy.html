Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

Overview

CTHours respects your privacy. This app is designed to store and manage your training hour records securely.

Data Collection

CTHours does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Information Stored

  • Training hour entries you create
  • Optional location data if you choose to log locations
  • Account information if you use Sign in with Apple

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, your data may be securely stored in your private iCloud account.

Third-Party Services

CTHours may use Apple services such as:

  • Sign in with Apple
  • StoreKit for subscriptions
  • iCloud for optional sync

No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are used.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: cthoursapp@gmail.com
