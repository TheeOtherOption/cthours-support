Common Questions

Subscription / Pro

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.
  • Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Manage or cancel anytime: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • To restore a prior purchase: Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases
  • Refunds are handled by Apple through the "Report a Problem" flow in your purchase history.

Data & Privacy

CTHours stores your data on your device and in your private iCloud account (if enabled). We do not maintain a developer-hosted database. To delete your data, delete the app or remove it from iCloud Storage in iOS Settings.

Location Services

If location suggestions don't appear, enable Location Services for CTHours: Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
